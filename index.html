Who we are

The growth of technology and digital skills in recent years has led to high number of individuals in the world training, learning and advancing their skills and talents to match up to the digital age that we are in. People seek for knowledge in programming, IT, coding, digital marketing, content creating and lots more. There are vast number of opportunities in the digital world that is now incorporated into every other industry there is from Economics to education to oil gas, every other industry.

There are now so many individuals in the world presently who are equipped with digital skills that a lot of them are underdogs due to the lack of experience, opportunity, right resources and invisibility. It is due to this problem, that a group of like minded individuals with experience in the digital world decided to establish WAVEHUB.

WaveHub is an innovative co-working space and skill development platform geared towards building groundbreaking solutions that aid the development of the African continent. Our key activities are; Co-working Space, Shared offices, Incubation and Bootcamps. This is a community that is open for all digital personnel -bloggers, content creators, digital marketers, programmers, coders- to come, rent a working space and meet people in the same industry as they are. Members of this community will be open to job opportunities, job collaborations and trainings.

The goal of Wavehub is to create a community of digital individuals skilled enough to provide aid and solutions to digital problem.
